The problem

Trip planning is a wall of tabs.

Flight sites. Hotel sites. Reviews. A group chat arguing about dates. A spreadsheet nobody updates.

Then a flight breaks three hours before boarding, and every one of them goes quiet. You're the one on hold.

The gap

Talking is easy.
Acting is hard.

Booking a trip and fixing it when it breaks means being trusted to spend money and make a phone call on someone's behalf.

Nobody has cracked that, because the trust problem is real: how do you let an AI act for you without it ever going rogue?

Meet Waypoint

"We just landed. Find us ramen."
"My flight got delayed. Figure it out."
